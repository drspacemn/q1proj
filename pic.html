In this exercise you will be given an english sentence that has a figurative meaning known as an idiom.

example: It's raining cats and dogs.

meaning: It is raining very hard.

In order to figure out these idioms you will be given a picture instead of selected words and will have to guess the correct idiom

If you would like another picture select the button

If you enter the wrong answer you will be provided the meaning of the idiom
